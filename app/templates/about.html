About HealthPredict

Bridging the gap between advanced machine learning and accessible preventative healthcare.

Our Mission

Heart disease and diabetes are among the leading causes of mortality worldwide. HealthPredict aims to provide a fast, accessible, and reliable preliminary risk assessment tool powered by artificial intelligence. By leveraging historical clinical data, we help users understand their risk profiles and make informed health decisions.
